FAQS

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our platform and services.

What makes your prediction engine different from other analytics platforms?

Our prediction engine stands out with its tiered approach to analysis. The Free tier offers reliable statistics using fundamental metrics. The Premium tier combines structured controls with custom factor weighting, allowing you to tailor predictions. The Ultra Premium tier offers a boundary-free prediction experience with customizable factors, natural language input, and our highest accuracy rates (85-95%).

How accurate are your predictions?

Our prediction accuracy varies by tier: Premium (70-85%) and Ultra Premium (85-95%). These rates are based on extensive backtesting against historical data and are continuously validated against actual outcomes. Our system also provides confidence ratings for each prediction to give you transparency about expected reliability.

Can I switch between prediction tiers after subscribing?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your subscription tier at any time. Upgrades take effect immediately, while downgrades will apply at the beginning of your next billing cycle. We offer prorated billing when upgrading mid-cycle to ensure you only pay for what you use.

Which sports and leagues do you support?

We currently support all major North American and European leagues, including NBA, NFL, MLB, NHL, Premier League, La Liga, Serie A, and Bundesliga. We also cover major tournaments like the Champions League, World Cup, and Olympics. Our roadmap includes expanding to additional sports like tennis, golf, F1, and more based on user demand.

What is the "Boundless Predictions" feature in Ultra Premium?

Boundless Predictions is our most advanced interface that removes all structural constraints from the prediction process. Instead of predetermined factors or formats, you can input literally anything imaginable using natural language - from game stats and player movements to weather conditions, crowd energy, coaching decisions, or psychological factors. Our advanced AI processes these inputs with unparalleled freedom to deliver the most accurate predictions possible (85-95% accuracy).
